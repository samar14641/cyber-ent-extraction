matching chars: .^$*+?{}[]\|()

a, X, 9 ...: ordinary chars

. any char except newline i.e. \n
^ start
$ end
[] character class
	[ax4$] any of a, x, 4, or $ (special nature stripped in char class)
	[a-z] any lowercase
	[^5] any char except 5
	[5^] any of 5 or ^
\w any alphanum char or _ i.e. [a-zA-Z0-9_]
\W any non-alphanum char i.e. [^a-zA-Z0-9_]
\d any digit char i.e. [0-9]
\D any non-digit char i.e. [^0-9]
\s any space char i.e. [\t\n\f\v\r]
\S any non-space char i.e. [^\t\n\f\v\r]
\b any word boundry
\B any non word boundry

eg. [\s,.] any space, ',' or . char

+ 1 or more occurrences of the pattern on the left
	ca+t matches cat, caat, caaat ...
* 0 or more occurrences of the pattern on the left
	ca*t matches ct, cat, caat, caaat ...
	a[bcd]*b against 'abcbd' matches abcb
? 0 or 1 occurrences of the pattern on the left
	abc-?def matches abcdef or abc-def
{m, n} between m and n reps of the pattern (inclusive)
	ab{2, 4}c matches abbc, abbbc, and abbbbc
